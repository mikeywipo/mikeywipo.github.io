What's the difference between Margin, Border and Padding?

You should think of these like layers of clothing on a winters day. You are the CONTENT centre. PADDING would be the t-shirt that you wear. BORDER would be your Jersey and finally MARGIN would be your Jacket. Great! now you're ready to go outside. Just like your clothing layers you can add values to the thickness of each depending on the look you want.
