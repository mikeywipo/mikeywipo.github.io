Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

Growing up maori I've experienced the value of whakawhanaungatanga. By definition it means. 1. (noun) relationship, kinship, sense of family connection - a relationship through shared experiences and working together which provides people with a sense of belonging. It develops as a result of kinship rights and obligations, which also serve to strengthen each member of the kin group. This value has giving me a sense of belonging and identity.
